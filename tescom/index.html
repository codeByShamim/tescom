YOUR QUESTIONS ANSWERED IN OUT EXTENSIVE FAQ SECTION

What types of bicycles do you offer?
At our bicycle store, we offer a wide range of bicycles, including mountain bikes, road bikes, hybrid bikes, and city bikes. We cater to both beginners and experienced cyclists. Our goal is to provide you with the perfect bike to suit your riding style and preferences. Visit us today and let our team help you find your ideal ride!
How do I choose the right size bicycle for me?
Choosing the right bicycle size is crucial for a comfortable and efficient riding experience. We have a comprehensive sizing guide available on our website that will help you determine the correct bike size based on your height and inseam measurements. If you have any doubts, feel free to reach out to our customer support team for personalized assistance.
Do your bicycles come assembled?
Yes, we ship our bicycles partially assembled to ensure safe transportation. Upon receiving your bike, you will need to perform some simple assembly tasks, such as attaching the front wheel, handlebars, and pedals. We provide detailed instructions and video guides to help you with the assembly process.
DWhat warranty do you offer on your bicycles?
We stand by the quality of our bicycles and offer a comprehensive warranty to cover manufacturing defects and faulty components. Our standard warranty period is one year, but for certain models, we provide extended warranties. Please refer to our warranty policy on the website for more details.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to several countries. The shipping cost and delivery time may vary depending on the destination. During the checkout process, you can select your country, and the shipping options and fees will be displayed accordingly.
